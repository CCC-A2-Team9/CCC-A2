Scenario 1

Introduction

As for scenario 1, we try to explore whether the the number of employed people in Melbourne on AURIN is positively correlated with the number of tweets that talk about recruitment with a positive attitude.

The main reason for choosing this scenario is that employment is one of the most important metrics people are concerned about when they plan and decide to settle down somewhere via considering livability.

Hypothesis

For the 11 suburbs in the city of Melbourne, the number of recruitment tweets towards positive attitude is positively correlated with the the number of employed people from AURIN.

Regression Result

y=0.117035x+0.001543

y = the percentage of the number of employed people

x = the percentage of tweets that talk about recruitment towards a positive attitude

Conclusion

For the 11 suburbs in the city of Melbourne, the number of recruitment tweets towards positive attitude is positively correlated with the the number of employed people from AURIN.
